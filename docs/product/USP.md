# Unique Selling Propositions (USP)

> Status: v0.1 — draft for naming & positioning. Living document.

## One-liner

**The open-source food + fitness tracker where the community — not a corporation — owns the data: log macros, scan for allergens like gluten, and plan workouts, all in one free app.**

## Core USPs

1. **Allergen-safe by design.** Every product carries allergen data (gluten first, extensible to other allergies). One scan answers "can I eat this?" — with a community trust score instead of an unverified guess.
2. **Food + fitness in one app.** No more "how active are you?" guessing: your logged workouts inform your energy needs. Use either half of the app or both.
3. **Community-governed truth.** A trust graph weights contributions: reliable users count more, trolls converge to zero. Product entries are versioned; users browse and like versions. Reports are resolved by community majority vote — the app runs without developer interference.
4. **Free and open source (AGPL-3.0).** No paywall creep, ever. Barcode scanning, unlimited workouts, macro goals: free. Only future AI photo-logging (with real infra cost) may be paid.
5. **Frictionless logging.** Scan → not found → one tap to create a product with sensible portions ("1 burger = 150 g", not 1 gram). Recent foods across all meals. Count up or down — your choice. Flexible meal structure with unlimited snacks.
6. **Bilingual from day one.** Dutch and English, seeded with NEVO (Dutch national food database) and Open Food Facts.

## Positioning vs. competitors

| Competitor | Their weakness we exploit |
|---|---|
| MyFitnessPal | Paywalled scanning, database rot, hated 2026 redesign |
| Eetmeter | One macro at a time, painful product creation, no recents |
| FatSecret | Recents fragmented per meal |
| Yazio / Lifesum | Paid tiers not worth the price |
| Strong / Hevy | 3-workout cap (Strong); no nutrition side |
| Gluten scanner apps | Unverified crowd data, false positives/negatives, no trust system |

## Brand values (input for naming)

- Trust & transparency (open source, versioned data, trust graph)
- Community & democracy (votes, no dictator-developer)
- Safety (allergen confidence)
- Wholeness (food + fitness + body, one app)
- Simplicity (fast logging, count-up)
